We see the best in each other, our students, and our neighborhood. We work extremely hard to build something that we believe will leave the world better than how we found it - and we have a pretty good time along the way.

Here is more of what we're looking for:



    Happy people.

    We like our work, we like each other and we like working together.

    Doers.

    Building great things takes serious hustle. We're dreamers, sure - but we're doers first. We're fans of Yoda here: "Do or Do not. There is not try."

    Versatility.

    We're a startup - so comfort with ambiguity, the abilites to learn and adjust quickly, to be relentlessly solution- oriented, and to wear many hats at once are all crucial. Must be able to a great team player, but also take complete ownership over big projects. In short - we want entrepreneurs.

    Community Builders.

    We love and genuinely believe in the potential of our community. We are for and of the South Bronx.
